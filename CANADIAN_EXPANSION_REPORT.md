# Canadian Expansion Report - MySeniorValet
**Date**: August 1, 2025  
**Platform**: MySeniorValet - North America's Senior Living Transparency Platform

## Executive Summary
MySeniorValet has successfully expanded into Canada, adding 8 premier senior living communities across 4 provinces. This marks our first international expansion, transforming MySeniorValet from a U.S.-only platform to a comprehensive North American senior living resource.
