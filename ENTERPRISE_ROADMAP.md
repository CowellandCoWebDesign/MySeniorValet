## 🏗️ Architecture Highlights

### "Both Sides of the Fence" Philosophy
Every feature works bidirectionally:
- **Communities**: Full management and editing capabilities
- **Families**: Real-time viewing and communication access (FREE)

### Key Integration Points
- **Community Dashboard**: `/community-dashboard-enhanced`
- **Family Portal**: `/family-collaboration-center`
- **API Routes**: All registered in `server/routes/index.ts`

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Backend**: Express.js, PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM
- **AI**: Perplexity (primary), Claude (secondary), ChatGPT (backup)
- **Payments**: Stripe
- **Real-time**: WebSockets
