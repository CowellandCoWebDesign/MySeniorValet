🧠 AMAZON AFFILIATE DIRECTIVE – COMPLIANCE + ENRICHMENT

=== PART 1: AFFILIATE LINK RULES ===

1. ✅ All Amazon affiliate links must use the full, stable format:
   https://www.amazon.com/dp/ASIN?tag=myseniorvalet-20

2. ❌ Never use:
   - Shortened links (e.g., amzn.to/xyz) without first expanding
   - Any link without ?tag=myseniorvalet-20
   - Links to category pages, search results, or the Amazon homepage

3. ✅ The affiliate tag (?tag=myseniorvalet-20) must always be present and unaltered.
   - Do not strip, reorder, or modify the URL once verified.

4. ❌ Do not show or store:
   - Product prices or availability (unless pulled via Amazon PA-API)
   - Customer reviews or review counts
   - Full product descriptions (verbatim from Amazon)
   - Amazon-hosted images or media (unless embedded using approved tools)

5. ✅ Display of affiliate links is only allowed on public web pages.
   - Do NOT include Amazon affiliate links in emails, PDFs, or push notifications.
