**Current State**: Basic AI recommendations and search insights
**Enhancement Opportunity**: Advanced predictive modeling for care progression

**Implementation Plan**:
- **Care Progression Predictor**: AI analysis of medical conditions to predict future care needs (6-24 months)
- **Financial Planning Assistant**: Predictive modeling for care costs over time
- **Health Risk Assessment**: Integration with medical data for community matching

**Technical Requirements**:

**Business Impact**: High - Positions MySeniorValet as essential long-term planning tool
