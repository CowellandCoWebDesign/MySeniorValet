## Key Findings

1. **North American Focus**: Platform exclusively covers North America (US, Canada, Puerto Rico)
2. **Strong US Presence**: 89% of communities are in the United States
3. **Complete Canadian Coverage**: All provinces and territories represented
4. **Texas Dominance**: Texas has the most communities (4,165) but also the most missing coordinates
5. **HUD Integration**: 17.4% of communities have verified HUD pricing data
6. **Room for Expansion**: No coverage in Mexico, Central/South America, Europe, or Asia

## Recommendations for Global Expansion

1. **Complete US Coverage**: Focus on filling gaps in missing states
2. **Enhance Coordinate Data**: Priority on Texas, Ohio, California, Arizona, Idaho
3. **Mexico Expansion**: Natural next step for North American coverage
4. **European Markets**: UK, Germany, France have aging populations
5. **Asia-Pacific**: Japan, Australia, Singapore have strong senior care sectors
6. **Data Partnerships**: Establish relationships with international government agencies

- US Government databases (HUD, CMS, state licensing)
