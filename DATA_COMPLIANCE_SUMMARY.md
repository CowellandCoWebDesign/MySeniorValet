## Golden Rule Adherence

### ✅ SAFE SOURCES (What We Use)
- Government open data portals (federal, state, local)
- Official regulatory databases
- Opt-in paid APIs with proper authentication (Google Places, Unsplash)
- Public records and licensing databases

### 🚫 PROHIBITED SOURCES (What We Don't Touch)
- Private senior living directory websites requiring scraping
- Terms-locked business directories
- Social media platforms
- Review sites without API access
- Any source requiring terms acceptance for automated access
