## AI Enrichment Impact

### Current Challenge
Even after standardization, AI enrichment still faces matching issues because:
1. Database names may not exactly match online marketing names
2. Facilities may be listed under parent company names online
3. Regional variations in naming conventions

### Recommended Next Steps

1. **Implement Fuzzy Matching**
   - Use similarity scoring for name matching
   - Search for variations (e.g., "Atria Burlingame" vs "Atria Senior Living at Burlingame")

2. **Create Alias Mapping**
   - Map database names to known marketing variations
   - Build synonym dictionary for common chains

3. **Enhanced Search Strategy**
   - Search by address instead of name when possible
   - Use parent company + city as fallback search

4. **Continuous Monitoring**
   - Regular data quality audits
   - Automated cleanup on new entries
   - Track AI enrichment success rates
