# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Public location landing pages
- Public community detail pages
- Public resource, provider, vendor, directory, and informational pages
- Crawlability assets (`robots.txt`, sitemaps, favicon, social metadata, structured data)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated dashboards (`/dashboard/**`, `/family-**`, `/resident-**` when authenticated context is required)
- Admin pages (`/admin/**`, `/admin-mega-dashboard`, `/super-admin/**`)
- Internal API routes except where they directly affect crawlability (for example sitemap generation)

## Target audience
- Families researching senior living communities, care types, pricing, and related senior services.

## Primary keywords
- senior living
- assisted living
- memory care
- independent living
- senior living pricing
- senior living near me
- senior care resources
